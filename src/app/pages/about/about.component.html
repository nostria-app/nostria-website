About Nostria

Building the future of decentralized social communication on Nostr

Our Mission

Nostria was founded with a clear purpose: to make the Nostr protocol accessible and powerful for everyone. We believe in a future where social communication is decentralized, censorship-resistant, and owned by users—not corporations.

Our mission is to provide the most user-friendly, reliable, and feature-rich Nostr client, backed by robust infrastructure that helps the network thrive. We're committed to building tools that empower individuals and communities to connect freely while maintaining control of their data and online presence.

Our Story

2022

The Beginning

Before Nostria, there was Notes. A feature rich Nostr client that we built. Our experience with Notes taught us the importance of user experience and the need for a reliable infrastructure to support decentralized communication.

2023

Building and Growing

This was the year that more features was added to Notes. A lot of work went into making it a great client that performed well. Unfortunately there was no plan for monetization of Notes, and all development was done on voluntary contributions by developers.

2024

Ariton

The team working on Nostria today, built a Super App called Ariton. Ariton was built on Web 5 protocol, not on Nostr protocol.


Unfortunately, the Web 5 project was shut down by Block (mother company of TBD). This was a big blow to the team, but we learned a lot from the experience.

2025

Launching Nostria

Recognizing the need for reliable infrastructure to support the growing Nostr network, we launched Nostria. Nostria is designed to help the Nostr network scale, by building a modern Nostr client that does not rely on large centralized relays.


Building the Nostria app itself is not enough. We also needed to build a reliable infrastructure of relays and media servers to support the Nostr network.

Our Story

2022

The Beginning

Nostria started as a passion project by a small team of developers who saw the potential of Nostr as a decentralized communication protocol. The first prototype was built during a weekend hackathon, focusing on a clean, intuitive interface.

2023

Growing the Team

As interest in Nostr grew, so did our team. We brought on specialists in UX design, distributed systems, and infrastructure to create a truly robust client. Our first stable release launched in mid-2023, quickly gaining popularity for its reliability.

2024

Premium Infrastructure

Recognizing the need for reliable infrastructure to support the growing Nostr network, we launched Nostria Premium. This service provided dedicated relays, media hosting, and discovery services for users who needed more than the basic protocol offerings.

2025

Global Expansion

Today, Nostria is used by hundreds of thousands of people worldwide. We've expanded our infrastructure to data centers on every continent, ensuring fast and reliable service for all our users, regardless of location. Our team continues to grow as we build new features and improve the Nostr experience.

Our Principles

The values that guide everything we do

User Sovereignty

We believe users should own their data and have full control over their online presence. Nostria is designed to empower, not exploit.

Privacy First

Privacy is a fundamental right. We build with privacy as a core feature, not as an afterthought or marketing claim.

Open Development

Nostria is built in the open. We contribute to the Nostr protocol and share our improvements with the wider community.

Beautiful Experiences

Technical excellence should be matched with beautiful design. We create interfaces that are a joy to use every day.

Community Focused

We build for our community and with our community. User feedback drives our roadmap and shapes our priorities.

Global Accessibility

Communication tools should work for everyone. We design for users across cultures, languages, and accessibility needs.

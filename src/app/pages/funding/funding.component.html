Investment FAQs

Common questions about investing in Nostria

How does the 10% revenue share work?

Pre-Seed investors collectively receive 10% of all Nostria revenue. Your share is proportional to your investment amount relative to total Pre-Seed funds raised. Payments continue until you've received 120% of your initial investment.

What is the Angor protocol?

Angor is a P2P funding protocol built on Bitcoin that enables transparent milestone-based funding. It ensures funds are released only when development milestones are reached and verified.

What happens after the Pre-Seed round?

After the 3-month development period, we'll announce our Seed Round. Pre-Seed investors will have pro-rata rights to participate in the Seed Round and maintain their ownership percentage.

What are the minimum and maximum investment amounts?

The minimum investment in the Pre-Seed round is $1,000. There is no maximum, but investments above $50,000 require direct discussion with our team for custom terms.

How will I receive investment updates?

All investors receive monthly progress reports. Angel tier ($5,000+) and Partner tier ($10,000+) investors also get access to quarterly investor calls with the founding team.

When will revenue sharing begin?

Revenue sharing begins as soon as Nostria starts generating revenue from premium subscriptions, expected within 3 months after the completion of Pre-Seed development.

What is Pre-seed funding?

Pre-seed funding is the earliest investment a startup receives. Learn more here: Pre-seed Funding

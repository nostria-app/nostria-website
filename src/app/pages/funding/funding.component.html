How Revenue Sharing Works

Understanding your monthly payments and the exit mechanism

calculate

Revenue Calculation

Gross Revenue: All subscription revenue from Apple App Store and Google Play (after platform fees) plus Bitcoin Lightning payments.

Net Revenue: Gross revenue minus Montenegro corporate taxes (9%) and applicable VAT.

Your Share: 0.0001% of Net Revenue per NOS token you hold.

schedule

Payment Schedule

Revenue sharing payments are made monthly within 10 days of month-end.

Payments can be received via:

  • Bitcoin Lightning (valued at EUR rate)
trending_up

Exit Mechanism

The revenue sharing continues indefinitely until the exit threshold is reached.

Exit Threshold: When your cumulative payments reach €16 per token (4x the original €4 price).

After reaching the threshold, the company may terminate the agreement or negotiate new terms.

description

Transparency & Reporting

Monthly revenue reports are provided to all investors including:

  • Gross and Net Revenue calculations
  • App Store and Google Play reports
  • Bitcoin Lightning transaction logs
  • Tax documentation

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NOS tokens and revenue sharing

What are NOS tokens?

NOS tokens are RGB tokens issued on the Bitcoin blockchain by OpenResist foundation. Each token represents 0.0001% of Nostria's net revenue and entitles you to monthly revenue sharing payments. They are not equity or ownership in the company.

How much do NOS tokens cost?

Each NOS token costs €4 and must be purchased using Bitcoin or Bitcoin Lightning. The total supply is limited to 1,000,000 tokens.

How are revenue payments calculated?

You receive 0.0001% of Nostria's net revenue per token you hold. Net revenue is gross subscription revenue minus Montenegro corporate taxes (9%) and applicable VAT. Payments are made monthly.

What is the Bitcoin RGB protocol?

RGB is a smart contract system for Bitcoin that allows the creation of tokens on the Bitcoin blockchain. Your NOS tokens are stored in your Bitcoin wallet and can be verified on the blockchain.

How long do I receive payments?

Revenue sharing continues indefinitely until you reach the exit threshold of €16 per token (4x your investment). At that point, the company may terminate the agreement or negotiate new terms.

Can I sell my NOS tokens?

The Revenue-Based Financing Agreement restricts assignment of tokens without company consent. This is not a tradeable security but a revenue-sharing agreement.

What are the tax implications?

You are responsible for reporting revenue share payments as income in your tax jurisdiction. The company provides transaction records for your tax filings. Consult a tax professional for specific advice.

How is this different from equity investment?

This is a revenue-sharing agreement, not equity. You don't own any part of the company, have no voting rights, and can't influence business decisions. You simply receive a share of revenue.

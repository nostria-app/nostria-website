Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Nostria Premium

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Bitcoin/Lightning payments. We are in the process of incorporating credit/debit cards payments.

When available, you can pay through in-app purchases on iOS App Store or Google Play Store.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What is the Content Backup feature?

Once you subscribe to Nostria Premium, a new "Content Backup" section will appear in your sidebar menu, where you can manage all your backup settings, download backup locally, and restore previous content if needed.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Premium subscription at any time. Your benefits will continue until the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my media if I cancel Premium?

Your media remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation, giving you time to migrate if needed. You need to remove excess storage used above the free limit. After that period, content exceeding the free tier limit may be removed.

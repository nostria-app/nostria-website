About Nostria

Nostria is a modern, scalable Nostr client that makes decentralized social networking accessible to everyone while providing premium cloud infrastructure for power users.

Built for global scale, Nostria combines beautiful user experience with robust infrastructure, offering features like dedicated relays, media hosting, and automated backups to bridge the gap between decentralized principles and mainstream usability.

Nostria 2.0: A New Era for Decentralized Social Networking

Massive feature enhancements, performance optimizations, and overwhelming user acclaim mark this landmark release

December 29, 2025 – Nostria, a feature rich Nostr protocol client, today announced the release of Nostria 2.0, representing the most significant update in the platform's history. This milestone release delivers a comprehensive suite of new features, substantial performance improvements, and a refined user experience that has garnered unprecedented positive feedback from the Nostr community.

Transformative Updates Across the Platform

Nostria 2.0 represents months of intensive development, community feedback integration, and rigorous optimization. The release touches every aspect of the platform, from the core infrastructure to the user interface, delivering a cohesive experience that sets a new standard for Nostr clients.

Feature Highlights

  • Enhanced Media Support: Native support for images, videos, and audio with optimized loading and seamless playback across all platforms.
  • Advanced Search: Full-text search across your network with filters for content type, author, date range, and relay source.
  • Profile Enrichment: Expanded profile capabilities including badges, verification, and rich media banners.
  • List Management: Create and manage custom lists for organizing contacts, topics, and content streams.
  • Cross-Platform Sync: Seamless synchronization of settings, drafts, and preferences across all your devices.
  • Improved Zaps: Streamlined Lightning payments with detailed transaction history and recipient insights.
  • Offline Mode: Access your cached content and compose notes even without an internet connection. Music can also be saved for offline playback—perfect for flights or trips.

Premium Subscribers: Enhanced Value

Nostria Premium subscribers receive additional benefits with the 2.0 release. Premium features now have their own dedicated section in the app menu, making it easier to see and access all the benefits:

  • YouTube: Enjoy your favorite YouTube channels without distractions—a cleaner viewing experience built into Nostria
  • Analytics (Early Preview): Get better insights into how your posts are performing with detailed engagement metrics
  • Extended Media Storage: Increased storage limits for images and videos
  • Priority Support: Direct access to the Nostria support team

Premium subscriptions continue to be payable via Bitcoin Lightning Network, maintaining Nostria's commitment to decentralized payment infrastructure, and you can gift Premium to others directly within the app by using zaps.

April 1, 2026 Launch Announcement

Nostria Launches on Product Hunt

The modern Nostr client invites the Product Hunt community to explore a calmer social network built around ownership, portability, and real human connection.

April 1, 2026 - Nostria today announced its upcoming Product Hunt launch, scheduled for April 6th, 2026 at 12:01 AM PDT, bringing its modern Nostr client to a broader community of makers, early adopters, and people looking for a better social experience. Available across web, Windows, iOS, and Android, Nostria helps people reconnect with friends, discover media, and keep control of their identity without ads or surveillance.

Why Product Hunt

Product Hunt is where people go to find products that challenge stale defaults. For Nostria, this launch is an opportunity to meet users who care about thoughtful design, user ownership, and software that feels useful instead of extractive.

The Product Hunt listing introduces Nostria with a simple promise: "Your Social Network! Take control of your social experience." That message matches the product itself. Nostria is built on the open Nostr protocol so people can keep their identity, move between apps, and avoid being locked into a single platform.

Built for People Tired of Fragmented Social Apps

On Product Hunt, the Nostria team describes a familiar frustration: social life has been scattered across too many focused apps, each with different interfaces, different rules, and different companies controlling the experience. Nostria takes the opposite approach. It brings discovery, conversation, media, and sharing into one place while keeping user identity portable through Nostr.

The result is a product that feels more coherent without becoming another walled garden. Nostria aims to make decentralized social networking easier to adopt by focusing on experience first while preserving the freedoms that make open protocols matter.

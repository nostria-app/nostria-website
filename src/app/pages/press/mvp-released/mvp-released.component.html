Nostria MVP Officially Launches: The Beginning has Started

Decentralized Social Network Platform Goes Live with Full Support and Backwards Compatibility

Nostria, the innovative Nostr protocol client designed for global scale, today announced the official launch of its Minimum Viable Product (MVP), marking a pivotal milestone in decentralized social networking. As planned, the MVP was completed on August 31st, 2025, transitioning from development phase to a fully supported platform with guaranteed backwards compatibility going forward.

The Beginning has Started

"The Beginning has Started" represents more than just a product launch—it signifies the start of Nostria's mission to bring decentralized social networking to the masses. After months of open development where anyone was free to test and provide feedback, Nostria now stands as a complete, production-ready platform ready to serve users worldwide.

"Today marks a crucial milestone in our journey," said Sondre Bjellås, Founder and CEO of Nostria. "We're not just launching a product—we're beginning our mission to reach one million daily active users and prove that decentralized social media can compete with and surpass traditional platforms. This is only the beginning."

Built in the Open, Ready for Scale

Unlike traditional tech companies that develop behind closed doors, Nostria has been built transparently in the public eye. This open development approach has allowed the community to contribute feedback, test features, and help shape the platform throughout its development cycle.

The MVP represents a stable foundation with full backwards compatibility guarantees, ensuring that users and developers can build upon Nostria with confidence. The platform will continue to evolve with daily updates and improvements, maintaining the rapid development pace that has characterized the project since its inception.

Premium Features Powered by Bitcoin Lightning

Nostria Premium is now available, featuring Bitcoin Lightning Network integration for seamless, instant payments. Premium subscribers gain access to enhanced features including dedicated relays, premium media hosting, verified usernames, and advanced backup services—all designed to provide enterprise-grade reliability while maintaining decentralization principles.

"We believe in providing value at every tier," explained Sondre Bjellås. "Our free tier offers a complete social networking experience, while Premium users get the infrastructure and features needed for professional use. The Lightning integration makes subscribing as simple as scanning a QR code."

Ambitious Goals: One Million Daily Active Users

Nostria is being developed with mass adoption in mind. The platform's goal is ambitious but achievable: reaching one million daily active users by focusing relentlessly on user experience, performance, and feature completeness that rivals centralized alternatives.

The platform's architecture is designed to scale globally while maintaining the decentralized principles that make Nostr special. By distributing users across appropriate relays and providing premium infrastructure options, Nostria creates a faster, more reliable network that can support mainstream adoption.

Getting Started is Simple

One of Nostria's key advantages is how quickly new users can get started. The onboarding process has been designed to be intuitive and welcoming, removing the technical barriers that have traditionally made decentralized social networks intimidating for mainstream users.

The video demonstrates Nostria's commitment to user experience, showing how new users can join the decentralized social network without needing technical knowledge about relays, cryptographic keys, or blockchain technology. This ease of use is crucial for achieving the goal of one million daily active users.

About Nostria

Nostria is a next-generation client for the Nostr protocol, designed to make decentralized social networking accessible to everyone. Founded by experienced developers with deep expertise in distributed systems, Nostria focuses on user experience, global scalability, and premium infrastructure services. The platform is available across web, desktop, and mobile platforms.

With the completion of its MVP, Nostria now offers a complete, production-ready platform with full backwards compatibility and daily updates. The platform supports both free and premium tiers, with Premium subscriptions available through Bitcoin Lightning Network payments.

Learn more at www.nostria.app

About Nostr

Nostr (Notes and Other Stuff Transmitted by Relays) is a decentralized social networking protocol that enables censorship-resistant communication and social interaction. Unlike traditional social media platforms, Nostr gives users control over their data and social connections through cryptographic keys and distributed relay networks.

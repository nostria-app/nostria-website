October 6, 2025 Funding Announcement

Nostria Launches $400,000 Seed Round via Revenue-Based Financing on Angor Protocol

Innovative Bitcoin-native funding structure offers investors direct revenue share without equity or tokens

October 6, 2025 – Nostria, the next-generation Nostr client platform, today announced the launch of its $400,000 seed funding round using a Revenue-Based Financing (RBF) structure recorded on the Angor protocol. This innovative approach allows investors to participate directly in Nostria's premium subscription revenue through Bitcoin-settled monthly distributions, without taking equity or issuing tokens.

Revolutionary Funding Model

Unlike traditional venture capital or token offerings, Nostria's RBF structure provides investors with a transparent, formula-driven share of the company's subscription revenue. Contributors receive 50% of net premium subscription revenue, distributed monthly in Bitcoin based on their weighted contribution percentage.

"We're pioneering a new way to fund decentralized social infrastructure," said Sondre Bjellås, CEO of Nostria. "By using the Angor protocol to record contributions and automate distributions, we're creating a fully transparent funding mechanism that aligns investor interests with our long-term success—without diluting ownership or creating speculative token dynamics."

Investment Structure & Benefits

The seed round offers three investment tiers:

  • Supporter ($1,000): RBF agreement with 2× minimum review threshold, pro-rata rights, and 6 months of Nostria Premium
  • Angel ($5,000): All Supporter benefits plus quarterly investor calls, app credits recognition, and 1 year of Premium access
  • Partner ($10,000+): All Angel benefits plus direct team access, product roadmap input, and 1 year of Premium+

Pre-seed investors who contributed $38,000 earlier receive a 1.15× weighting uplift in the combined pool, recognizing their early support.

Angor Protocol Integration

The Angor protocol provides a Bitcoin-native platform for transparent project funding. By recording contributions on Angor, Nostria ensures:

  • Immutable record of all investor contributions and weightings
  • Transparent tracking of revenue distributions
  • Built-in accountability without intermediaries
  • Bitcoin-native settlement for all revenue shares

Growing Nostr Ecosystem

Nostr (Notes and Other Stuff Transmitted by Relays) is a decentralized social protocol experiencing rapid growth, with over 10 million accounts created and increasing mainstream adoption. Nostria differentiates itself through:

  • Premium infrastructure including dedicated media servers and discovery relays
  • Cross-platform support (Web, Windows, iOS, Android)
  • Intuitive user experience designed for mainstream adoption
  • Sustainable revenue model through premium subscriptions

The company projects strong revenue growth as the Nostr ecosystem expands and more users seek premium features for professional and creator use cases.

Use of Funds

The $400,000 seed funding will be allocated to:

  • Product Development (45%): Mobile app refinement, advanced features, and UX improvements
  • Infrastructure (30%): Scaling relay network, media servers, and premium services
  • Marketing & Growth (15%): User acquisition, creator partnerships, and brand development
  • Operations (10%): Team expansion, legal compliance, and administrative costs

About Nostria

Nostria is a premium Nostr client platform designed to make decentralized social networking accessible to mainstream users. With a focus on user experience, performance, and creator tools, Nostria provides the infrastructure and features needed for professional use of the Nostr protocol. The platform operates on a sustainable freemium model, offering premium features and infrastructure to power users while keeping the core experience free and open.

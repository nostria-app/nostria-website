The Solution

Trust your network, not an algorithm.

The Web of Trust filters your feed through the people you already follow — and the people they trust. No central authority. No opaque AI. Just human relationships, cryptographically verified.

  • See content from people your network vouches for
  • Automatically hide spam, bots & strangers
  • Fully transparent — you control the trust depth
  • Works across the entire Nostr protocol
Activate in seconds

One toggle. Instant trust.

Turn on Web of Trust in a single click. Nostria builds your trust graph from the people you already follow — no setup, no friction, no compromise.

  • Enable from Settings → Web of Trust
  • Tune the trust ranking threshold to your taste
  • Filter feeds, mentions, and notifications
